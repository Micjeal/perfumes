How to Choose Your Perfect Scent

A simple guide to finding your signature fragrance

1

Know the Fragrance Families

Understand different fragrance categories like floral, oriental, woody, and fresh to identify what appeals to you.

2

Consider the Occasion

Daytime scents are typically lighter, while evening scents can be more intense and sensual.

3

Test on Your Skin

Fragrances react differently with each person's skin chemistry. Always test before buying.

4

Take Your Time

Let the fragrance develop on your skin for a few hours before making a decision.
